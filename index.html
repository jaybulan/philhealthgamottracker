Help & Support

Standalone Application
This is a standalone tool that runs entirely offline. No internet connection is required after download.

Please download the latest version to ensure you are up to date with the most current facility and medicine data.

An advisory will be issued by PhilHealth Regional Office II when a new version is released.

How to use the GAMOT Tracker:

  1. Search Medicine (GAMOT) — Type the name of the medicine you need. Only facilities that carry it will appear.
  2. Filter by Accreditation Status — Select All, GAMOT, or YAKAP to filter by accreditation type.
  3. Filter by Province — Select or type your province to narrow down facilities in your area.
  4. Filter by Municipality — Further filter by your municipality or city.
  5. Click a Facility Card — Tap any facility to expand its full details including address, contact number, email, authorized physician, and accreditation status.
